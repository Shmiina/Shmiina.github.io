Tips on How to Handle Police Corruption in the Philippines

According to a survey by the Global Corruption Barometer of the anti-corruption watchdog Transparency International, the most corrupt institution in the Philippines is the Philippine National Police. The survey covered approximately 114,000 respondents in 107 countries, with 69% of the respondents voting for the PNP as the most corrupt (Romero, 2013).
